Fitness helps you think and feel better.

Exercise improves mental health by reducing anxiety, depression, and negative mood and by improving self-esteem and cognitive function. Exercise has also been found to alleviate symptoms such as low self-esteem and social withdrawal.

Today is the Best Day to Start Training.

Between 2 p.m. and 6 p.m., your body temperature is at its highest. This may mean you'll be exercising during the window of time your body is most ready, potentially making it the most effective time of day to work out.

Simple Condition for all Around Fitness.

As you build muscle through weight training, your muscle fibers experience microscopic tears. These tears are part of the strength-training process and are often the cause of muscle soreness the day after your workout. As a result, your muscles may swell slightly and retain fluid for a few days after your workout.

OFF season is finished now what?.

When you first start exercises, your heart rate increases quickly, your body heats up, and your muscles fatigue rapidly. But once your body gets used to the new movement and activity level, you'll find that you're heart rate doesn't jump as quickly, you don't get as hot, and your muscles can work harder and for longer.
